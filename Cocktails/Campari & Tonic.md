## Ingredients
- 2 oz [[../Ingredients/Campari|Campari]]
- Top with [[../Ingredients/Tonic Water|Tonic Water]]

## Instructions
1. Fill glass with ice.
2. Add Campari and top with tonic.
3. Stir lightly and serve.
