## Ingredients
- 1.5 oz [[../Ingredients/Cachaça|Cachaça]]
- 0.75 oz [[../Ingredients/Sweet Vermouth|Sweet Vermouth]]
- 0.75 oz [[../Ingredients/Cynar|Cynar]]
- Dash of [[../Ingredients/Orange Bitters|Orange Bitters]]

## Instructions
1. Stir all ingredients with ice.
2. Strain into glass.
3. Serve neat or over a single cube.
