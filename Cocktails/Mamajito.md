## Ingredients
- 2 oz [[../Ingredients/Rum|Rum]]
- 5 [[../Ingredients/Mint|Mint]] Leaves
- Top with [[../Ingredients/Sprite Zero|Sprite Zero]]
- Garnish with [[../Ingredients/Lime|Lime]] Wedge

## Instructions
1. Muddle mint.
2. Add rum and ice.
3. Top with Sprite Zero, stir, and serve.
