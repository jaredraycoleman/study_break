## Ingredients
- 2 oz [[../Ingredients/Rum|Rum]]
- [[../Ingredients/Coke|Coke]] to top up
- Garnish with [[../Ingredients/Lime|Lime]] Wedge

## Instructions
1. Fill glass with ice.
2. Add rum, top with Coke.
3. Garnish and serve.
