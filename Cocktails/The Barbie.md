## Ingredients
- 2 oz [[../Ingredients/Gin|Gin]]
- 1 oz [[../Ingredients/Watermelon Liqueur|Watermelon Liqueur]]
- 0.75 oz [[../Ingredients/Simple Syrup|Simple Syrup]]
- Juice from 1 [[Lemon|Lemon]]

## Instructions
1. Shake all ingredients with ice.
2. Strain into glass.
3. Garnish and serve.
