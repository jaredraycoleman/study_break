## Ingredients
- 2 oz [[../Ingredients/Cachaça|Cachaça]]
- 1 [[../Ingredients/Lime|Lime]], sliced
- 2 tsp [[../Ingredients/Sugar|Sugar]]

## Instructions
1. Muddle lime slices with sugar in a glass.
2. Add ice and cachaça.
3. Stir well and serve.
